Our Story

ABOUT US

BUILT FOR THOSE WHO TAKE THEIR GAME SERIOUSLY

Real G's Golf was born from a simple frustration: finding premium golf gear shouldn't be complicated, overpriced, or boring. We're golfers who wanted access to serious equipment at fair prices, shipped fast, with no nonsense.

We source the best gear from around the world — clubs, balls, apparel and accessories — so you can focus on your game, not your kit. Whether you're shooting scratch or just getting started, we've got what you need to play like a Real G.

Based in Australia. Shipping worldwide. Built for the game.
