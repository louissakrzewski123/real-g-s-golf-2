PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information when you place an order. We also collect usage data such as pages visited and products viewed to improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • To respond to your enquiries
  • To send promotional emails (only if you opt in)
  • To improve our website and product offerings

Payment Information

All payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your shipping address with our fulfilment partners solely for the purpose of delivering your order.

Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your shopping cart and improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@realggsgolf.com.

Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at hello@realggsgolf.com or write to us at our registered address in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia.
