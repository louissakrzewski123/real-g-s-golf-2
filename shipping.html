SHIPPING POLICY

Last updated: January 2025

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–3 business days of payment confirmation. You will receive an email confirmation once your order has been dispatched.

Domestic Shipping (Australia)

Standard shipping within Australia takes 5–10 business days. Express shipping (3–5 business days) is available at checkout. Free standard shipping is available on orders over $150 AUD.

International Shipping

We ship worldwide. International orders typically take 10–21 business days depending on destination and customs processing. Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight.

Customs & Duties

International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees charged by their country. These charges are not included in our prices and are outside our control.

Tracking

A tracking number will be emailed to you once your order is dispatched. Please allow 24–48 hours for tracking information to update.

Lost or Damaged Parcels

If your order is lost or arrives damaged, please contact us at hello@realggsgolf.com within 14 days of the estimated delivery date. We will work with you to resolve the issue promptly.
